# Provenance: Coding-Learning Lesson Layout Research

Research date: 2026-07-12

The brief separates independently studied learning mechanisms from current
product observations and vendor claims. Ratings are time-, territory-, and
version-sensitive snapshots.

| Source | Type | Used for | Confidence / limitation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Freeman et al. 2014, PNAS, https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1319030111 | STEM meta-analysis | Active learning | High for course-level active learning; does not select an app layout |
| Agarwal et al. 2021, https://doi.org/10.1007/s10648-021-09595-9 | Classroom systematic review | Retrieval practice | High general evidence; programming-specific evidence is thinner |
| Roediger & Karpicke 2006, https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/16507066/ | Controlled study | Delayed retrieval benefit | Strong mechanism evidence using prose, not coding |
| Cepeda et al. 2006, https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/16719566/ | Meta-analysis | Spacing | High general memory evidence; exact intervals vary |
| Barbieri et al. 2023, https://doi.org/10.1007/s10648-023-09745-1 | Meta-analysis | Worked examples | Stronger general evidence, mostly mathematics |
| Atkinson et al. 2003, https://doi.org/10.1037/0022-0663.95.4.774 | Experiment | Faded assistance | Influential controlled evidence outside programming |
| Learning and Instruction 2025, https://doi.org/10.1016/j.learninstruc.2025.102142 | Meta-analysis | Expertise reversal | Current strong synthesis; assistance should adapt to prior knowledge |
| Rey et al. 2019, https://doi.org/10.1007/s10648-018-9454-0 | Meta-analysis | Segmenting | Supports learner-paced segments; no universal duration |
| Richter et al. 2016, https://doi.org/10.1016/j.edurev.2015.12.003 | Meta-analysis | Signaling | Small-to-medium effects with boundary conditions |
| Sundararajan & Adesope 2020, https://doi.org/10.1007/s10648-020-09522-4 | Meta-analysis | Coherence | Supports removing irrelevant detail |
| Multimedia-learning review 2022, https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s40561-022-00200-2 | Systematic review | Segmenting/modality caveats | Broad review; principles have context-sensitive conflicts |
| Shute 2008, https://doi.org/10.3102/0034654307313795 | Research review | Formative feedback | Strong general design guidance |
| Margulieux et al. 2020, https://doi.org/10.1186/s40594-020-00222-7 | CS1 quasi-experiment | Subgoal labels | Moderate; near-term gains and attrition, no average exam gain |
| Sentance et al. 2019, https://doi.org/10.1080/08993408.2019.1608781 | School quasi-experiment | PRIMM | Promising, nonrandom, small effect, not adult self-study evidence |
| Kumar 2013, https://doi.org/10.1145/2462476.2462507 | Programming intervention | Tracing/prediction | Limited transfer and study design constraints |
| Xie et al. 2018, https://par.nsf.gov/biblio/10107747 | Small randomized programming study | Tracing strategy | Promising and narrow; N=24 |
| Ericson et al. 2022, https://doi.org/10.1145/3571785.3574127 | Systematic literature review | Parsons problems | Useful scaffold; heterogeneous evidence and limited replication |
| Apple HIG Playing video, https://developer.apple.com/design/human-interface-guidelines/playing-video | Platform guidance | Familiar player controls/aspect ratio | Authoritative macOS design guidance, not learning-outcome evidence |
| Apple HIG Accessibility, https://developer.apple.com/design/human-interface-guidelines/accessibility | Platform guidance | Captions, alternatives, keyboard/VoiceOver | Authoritative accessibility guidance |
| W3C accessible media player, https://www.w3.org/WAI/media/av/player/ | Accessibility guidance | Keyboard and media controls | Authoritative web guidance, adapted to native app semantics |
| Scrimba, https://v1.scrimba.com/ | Official product surface | Editable screencast pattern | Vendor description/observation, no independent efficacy claim |
| Codecademy help, https://help.codecademy.com/hc/en-us/articles/360056641953-Accessibility-Guide | Official product help | Multi-pane lesson structure | Current product documentation |
| freeCodeCamp contributor docs, https://contribute.freecodecamp.org/how-to-work-on-coding-challenges/ | Official curriculum docs | Theory/workshop/lab/review pattern | Current curriculum structure |
| Hyperskill guide, https://hyperskill.org/guide | Official product guide | Theory/practice/project progression | Vendor observation; efficacy claims not treated as independent evidence |
| Khan Academy programming lesson, https://www.khanacademy.org/computing/computer-programming/programming/intro-toprogramming/v/programming-intro | Official public lesson | Player-first/transcript convention | Direct product observation |
| Frontend Masters course/player, https://frontendmasters.com/courses/complete-react-v9/ | Official public course surface | Player, transcript, notes, outline | Direct product observation |
| Apple App Store product pages for Mimo, Sololearn, Brilliant, Codecademy Go, Frontend Masters | Store listings | Rating snapshots and screenshots | Satisfaction/popularity only; not learning efficacy; values drift |

Research roles:

- learning-evidence researcher: primary studies, reviews, evidence hierarchy;
- product-layout researcher: current official product/store observations;
- verifier: claim/source/rating checks and overclaim removal;
- reviewer: design synthesis, contradiction and evaluation-gate review.
